How Communication Assistant works

  1. Pick a scenario and tone — the draft updates to match professional norms.
  2. Choose who you’re emailing from your courses (or your advisor).
  3. Edit the message, insert placeholders, and use chips to add helpful detail.
  4. Copy to your email client — nothing is sent automatically in this prototype.

Full communication guide

  • Open with a respectful greeting that matches your tone choice.
  • State your course and purpose in the first few sentences.
  • Ask one focused question per email when possible.
  • Close with appreciation and your full name / class year if appropriate.
  • Follow campus policies and instructor preferences from the syllabus.
